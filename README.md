 How Tax Filing Actually Works

Filing your taxes is essentially a math problem with two main parts:

1. Calculate Your Total Tax: Start with your total income, subtract deductions to find your taxable income, then apply tax rates. This gives you your tax liabilitythe amount you owe.
2. Apply What You've Already Paid & Credits: Subtract taxes already withheld from your paychecks. Then, apply tax credits (which directly reduce your tax bill). The final number is either your refund (you overpaid) or amount due (you underpaid).
